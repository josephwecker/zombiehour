The Controls:

Key+Key means those keys pressed simultaneously

Key, Key means those keys pressed successively

Directions

  • W: North
  • A: West
  • S: South
  • D: East
  • W+A: Northwest
  • W+D: Northeast
  • S+A: Southwest
  • S+D: Southeast

Commands

  • DirectionKey: Walk in direction
  • Walking into a zombie is a melee attack
  • j, DirectionKey: Shoot in direction
  • k, DirectionKey: Close door in direction
  • l, DirectionKey: Open door in direction
